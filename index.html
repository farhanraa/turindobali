Waisak(Vesak) Day

Vesak Day is a celebration to commemorate the birth of Buddha. There are three important stages in Buddhist life that are celebrated in the celebration, namely: the stage of birth, the stage of enlightenment, and also death. These three stages are commonly referred to as Trisuci Waisak, which based on the traditional calendar falls on the same day. This celebration is usually done at the peak of the first full moon in May each year. The Buddha himself was born in Lumbini, an area at the foot of the Himalayas currently entering the region of Nepal, where the Buddha originally held the Prince Siddharta was born and spent 29 years of his age.

Nyepi is a Hindu feast celebrated every year New Saka. Today it falls on the Tilem Kesanga (IX) count which is believed to be the day of purification of the gods at the center of the ocean carrying the essence of living water.

Festival Layang-Layang(Kite)

The Bali Kite Festival is an annual international kite festival held in July in Padang Galak, Sanur, Bali. The giant traditional kite (with a width of 4 meters and a length of 10 meters) was made and flown in a competition between teams from banjar in Denpasar. This seasonal festival has a religious message intended to send a message to the Hindu gods to provide abundant agricultural produce.
